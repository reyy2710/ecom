Exhibitions

Modern Masters: Contemporary Art in the 21st Century

"Modern Masters" is a dynamic exhibition showcasing the innovative works of contemporary artists from around the globe. The exhibition features a diverse range of media, including painting, sculpture, photography, and digital art, highlighting the unique perspectives and techniques that define modern art today.

Date and Time:
July 15, 2024 - August 31, 2024
Monday - Friday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday: 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM

Location:
